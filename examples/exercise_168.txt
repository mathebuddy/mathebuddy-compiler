EXERCISE Partielle Ableitungen
@code
let a = rand(0,1);
let b = 1 - a;
let c = rand(4,8);
let f(x,y) = c / (a*x^2 + b*y^2);
let fx(x,y) = diff(f, x);
let fy(x,y) = diff(f, y);
@text
Sei $"f"(x,y)=f$. Bestimme die **partiellen Ableitungen**:
- $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}=$ #fx
- $\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}=$ #fy
